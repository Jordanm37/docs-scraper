## How to convert images with Cyrillic characters

1. Take a sreenshot of your image using keyboard shortcuts: `ctrl` + `cmd` + `M` for Mac or `ctrl` + `alt` + `M` for Windows & Linux.
2. Your image is instantly digitized and copied to your clipboard.

## How to convert Cyrillic PDF

1. Go to [Snip web app](https://snip.mathpix.com).
2. Drag your PDF into the sidebar or directly into the document.
3. Get your [Markdown](/mathpix-markdown) result, which can be exported to DOCX, LaTeX, HTML, and Overleaf.

Your PDF document can contain text, math, tables, images, and chemical diagrams. It even works perfectly on two-column PDFs!

Snip is the perfect editing environment powered by [Mathpix Markdown](/mathpix-markdown). Convert PDFs to editable files or create your scientific documents by scratch. Any Snips you have taken with the mobile or desktop apps can be pasted into your document by selecting them from your Snips sidebar.
