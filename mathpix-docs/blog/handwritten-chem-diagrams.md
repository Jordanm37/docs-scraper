In addition to significant improvements to our printed chemical diagram OCR, we have also added support for converting handwritten chemical diagrams into canonical SMILES code.

You can take pictures of your chemistry notes directly on the mobile apps, sync to your desktop, and paste the SMILES into apps like ChemDraw. You can also use the **Search** tab to identify chemical diagrams.
