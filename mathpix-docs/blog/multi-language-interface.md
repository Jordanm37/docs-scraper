If you are a non-English speaker, you can now use [Mathpix Snip](https://snip.mathpix.com) on the web in your native language. Our existing support for 28 languages in image and PDF conversion is now complemented by app localization in 14 languages.

Mathpix currently supports English, Bengali, German, Spanish (Castilian), French, Hindi, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Russian, Urdu, Vietnamese, Chinese, and we’re working on adding more languages!

## How to change your display language?

1. **Go to Settings**: Open the web app and make your way to the Settings section in the bottom left-hand corner.

![Settings](/images/blog/multi-lang-blog1.webp)

2. **Language & Region**: Within the Settings, you’ll choose the “Language & Region” option.

![Language & Region](/images/blog/multi-lang-blog2.webp)

3. **Choose your language**: The list of language choices is presented via a dropdown menu. Simply select your desired language from the list.

![Dropwdown with list of languages](/images/blog/multi-lang-blog3.webp)
