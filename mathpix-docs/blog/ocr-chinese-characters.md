## How to convert images with Chinese

1. Take a sreenshot of your image using keyboard shortcuts: `ctrl` + `cmd` + `M` for Mac or `ctrl` + `alt` + `M` for Windows & Linux.
2. Your image is instantly digitized and the text is copied to your clipboard.
3. Edit the result in-app or paste it into your document!

## How to convert Chinese PDF

1. Go to [Snip](https://snip.mathpix.com).
2. Drag your PDF into the sidebar or directly into the document.
3. Get your [Markdown](/mathpix-markdown) result, which can be edited and exported to DOCX, LaTeX, HTML, and Overleaf.

Your PDF document can contain text, math, tables, images, and more. It even works on two-column PDFs!

In addition to converting Chinese documents, Mathpix can convert printed and handwritten English, French, Spanish, Russian, Hindi, Portuguese, and a lot more. Learn about our supported languages [here](/language-support).
