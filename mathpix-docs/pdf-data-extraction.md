## Our PDF Data Extraction tools

### Copy single lines

![Copy single lines of text and math]()

Hover over any line in the PDF (text or math) to reveal a copy to clipboard button to the left of the line.

![Copy single lines of text and math]()

### Select multiple lines

![Click and drag to select multiple lines]()

Click and drag to highlight a region of interest (i.e. multiple lines) or double click any line to get access to selectable and copyable text or LaTeX.

![Click and drag to select multiple lines]()

### Choose the format to copy equations and tables

![Copy math in different formats]()

You can choose different formats to copy and export equations and tables.

Hover over any equation or table to reveal a copy to clipboard button and choose the format to copy or export.

![Copy math in different formats]()
